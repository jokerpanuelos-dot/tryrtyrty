Mercy vs Vladimir — How to Play

A two-player turn-based card duel. Read this once and you're ready to battle.

🎯 What Is This Game?

This is a two-player card battle game played on a single screen. Each player controls a hero — Mercy or Vladimir — and takes turns playing cards, building their battlefield, and attacking the opponent. The goal is simple: reduce your opponent's HP from 20 to 0 before they do the same to you.

🧙 The Two Heroes

✦ Mercy — The Graceful Guardian

A healer and protector. Her cards focus on healing, shields, wards, and empathic strikes. She can restore HP, absorb damage, and outlast opponents through attrition. She starts first.

◆ Vladimir — The Iron Strategist

A cold tactician and bruiser. His cards deal raw damage, stuns, AoE hits, and debuffs. He can discard enemy cards, rally his troops, and trade HP for raw power.

🔄 How a Turn Works (3 Steps)

1

Draw Phase → Click "🃏 Draw Cards"

You draw 2 cards from your deck into your hand (hand limit: 5). You must draw before doing anything else. The "Draw Cards" button pulses to remind you. If you are stunned, you skip this phase.

2

Play Phase → Put cards onto the Battlefield

Drag cards from your hand to your field zone, or double-click them to play instantly. Each card costs 💎 Mana (the number in the top-right circle). You start with 3 Mana — it grows as the game goes on. Play as many cards as your mana allows. Only the active player can play cards.

3

Battle → Click "⚔ Attack" (optional)

All your field cards attack together once per turn. Damage formula: your total ATK − half the opponent's total DEF. Cards on the field stay there and attack again in future turns — build up your army! You can skip attacking and just End Turn if you prefer.

→

End Turn → Click "End Turn →"

Pass control to your opponent. Regen effects trigger. Mana resets (and grows each turn). You cannot end your turn without drawing first.

💎 Mana — The Resource

Each card has a Mana cost (number in the circle, top-right of the card). You start with 3 Mana per turn, gaining +1 every 2 turns up to a max of 7. Unspent mana does not carry over. Bigger, more expensive cards are more powerful — save up for them!

📊 Card Stats

  • ⚔ ATK — Damage this card contributes when you attack. Higher = hits harder.
  • 🛡 DEF — Reduces incoming damage. Opponent's total DEF is halved and subtracted from your ATK.
  • ⚡ SPD — Flavor/speed stat. "Swift" cards always strike effectively.
  • 💎 Cost — Mana needed to play the card from your hand.

🃏 Card Types

⚔ Attack

High ATK. Core damage dealers. Stack multiple for big hits.

🛡 Defense

High DEF. Reduces damage taken each turn you're attacked.

✦ Enchantment

Triggers an ongoing effect on play: healing, regen, buffs.

☀ Spell

One-time powerful effect: discard, destroy, ward, power boost.

⚡ Special Abilities

  • Heal — Restores HP immediately when the card is played.
  • Shield/Barrier — Absorbs a set amount of the next incoming damage.
  • Ward — Reduces the next attack's damage by a fixed number.
  • Regen — Heals you at the end of each of your turns automatically.
  • Pierce — Ignores part of the opponent's DEF when calculating damage.
  • Stun — Opponent skips their Draw Phase on their very next turn.
  • Halve — The next hit you take is halved before it applies.
  • Rally — All cards currently on your field permanently gain +2 ATK.
  • Discard — Forces opponent to randomly lose a card from their hand.
  • Destroy — Removes one of the opponent's field cards from the game.
  • Fortify — Doubles the DEF of all your current field cards.
  • Blood Pact — You lose 3 HP but your field cards gain +4 ATK permanently.
  • ULTIMATE — Bypasses ALL defense, wards, and shields. Full ATK hits directly.

📋 Quick Rules Cheat Sheet

  • Both players start at 20 HP and 3 Mana per turn.
  • Only the active player can draw and play cards. The opponent's hand is locked.
  • Cards on the field stay between turns — they accumulate and attack again.
  • You can play as many cards as your mana allows per turn.
  • You can skip attacking — just End Turn if you want to play defensively.
  • You must draw before ending your turn.
  • First player to hit 0 HP loses. Good luck!
